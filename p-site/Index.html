Why Mexico?

Mexico is one of the most visited countries in the world. It is a place where you can have beautiful experiences. You can visit beaches, explore old cities, visit pyramids, or even enjoy the traditions. Don’t you speak Spanish? No worries at all. You can visit beautiful places where you will find help with English. So, what are you waiting for?

Do you know?

  • Mexico has 32 states, which Chihuahua is the biggest one.
  • Even though the main culture is the same, throughout the 32 states, you will find slight differences such as food or traditional clothing.
  • The currency is the Mexican peso.
  • Check the weather before traveling. Some states are warmer than others.
  • Do not forget to say “Hola”, “Adios”, and “Gracias.”
