Content

Magic Places

Mexico is a land of opportunities and experiences. You can find sunsets, museums, beaches, pyramids, or even old cities. However, these magical places on this website are the key to an unforgettable trip to Mexico. They are unique worldwide, and definitely, everyone should explore these marvelous places.

Xochimilco

Xochimilco is a beautiful place located in the state of Mexico, specifically in southern Mexico City. It is a giant lake where you can rent a boat and enjoy the views and environment. It is also viral for being the habit of many axolotls, an amphibious originally from Mexico. The price for renting a boat is around 400 to 500 pesos / 20 to 25 USD per hour. The boats are famous for being decorated with so many colors and images. If you embark on this experience, do not forget to take pictures and enjoy the lake.

Pueblos Magicos

In Mexico, you can find various places to visit; however, the magical towns are one of the best experiences tourists could experience in Mexico. Mexico has 132 magical towns all over the country, and they are defined as historical places with symbolism. They are colorful, surrounded by nature, old-style buildings, delicious food, and many gifts and merchandise. If you want to experience nature, magical towns are all you need in your tip.

Traditions

Mexico is a land full of magic and traditions. Over the years, people worldwide have been fascinated with the culture in Mexico. Visiting Mexico, regardless of your stay, is an excellent opportunity to explore the marvels deeply that a country like Mexico can offer you. Experiences that will remain in your memory forever.

Dia de Muertos

Day of the dead is a celebration on November 1 and 2. The belief is people who passed away come on these days to visit their families and closest ones. They also eat and enjoy these days of celebration. This is the reason why people in Mexico visit cemeteries, decorate tombs, put food on altars and pictures of their loved ones who passed away. People usually paint their faces in the shape of skulls to remember their ancestors. This is an unforgettable experience that anyone should lose.

Traditional Dances

Mexico is highly known for its diversity, and culture and dances are not an exception. Every state in Mexico has the privilege of sharing traditions through folkloric dances. The clothes used in every traditional dance can vary. White clothes, skirts, and hats are the traditional colors for dances in the south of Mexico, such as Veracruz. On the other hand, places like Jalisco use more vivid colors. Dances are a crucial aspect that defines Mexican culture.

Facts

Mexico is full of interesting factors and legends. Each corner is full of adventure, and every state has something different to offer you. You can discover old traditions and stories that will amaze you. It is an exciting opportunity to connect with history and find stories to tell to your family and friends.

What is the meaning of the flag?

The beautiful flag of Mexico is a constant reminder of the history that occurred throughout the years in Mexico. The green part means hope and prosperity, the white part signifies purity, while the red part stands for the blood that the heroes of Mexico shed over the years. The eagle and snake are based on the Aztec symbol for Tenochtitlan, a city founded by Aztecs. The legend tells Aztecs found an eagle sitting on a cactus while devouring a serpent, and that was a signal they needed to build a city, Tenochtitlan.

Mexican Food

Mexican food is enough for travelers around the world to visit Mexico. Gastronomy in Mexico is diverse and delicious. The most popular dish is tacos; however, pozole, enchiladas, tostadas, tortas are other exquisite options. Using masa for many traditional dishes makes Mexican food simple, unique, and interesting for foreign travelers.
